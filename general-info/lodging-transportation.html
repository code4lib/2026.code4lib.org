Transportation

From the Airport

The Philadelphia International Airport is the closest major airport to the conference.

Public Transport

Trains depart every 30 mins from the airport towards Center City. However, this line does not go directly to the hotel, so you will need to transfer (including additional fare) to the Market-Frankford Line (MFL) at the Market & 11th (Jefferson) Station. Take th MFL eastbound towards Frankford to 2nd Street Station. The hotel is a two block walk from 2nd St Station.

Taxis

For rates to and from the airport as well as general taxi info, see the Philadelphia International Airport taxi information page.

See more transportation options on Google Maps.

From 30th St Station (Amtrak)

In 30th street station, follow the signs for the Market Frankford Line (MFL). Take the MFL Eastbound, towards Frankford, to 2nd St Station. The hotel is a two block walk from the station.

Taxis are also ready available at the station.

Parking

The conference hotel offers overnight valet parking.

Multiple nearby parking garages are availbale as well and may be cheaper.
